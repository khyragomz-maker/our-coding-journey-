What this project shows

This site demonstrates our learning: page structure (header, nav, main, footer), content organization, images, links, lists, tables, forms, multimedia embedding, CSS styling and simple interactivity.

(We learned how small tags can tell big stories.)

Why we chose this design

We used brown and cream tones because they feel warm and approachable. Fonts were chosen to mix friendly handwriting (Pacifico) and readable body text (Poppins / Merriweather for long paragraphs).
